Paper Soccer
how to play

Paper soccer is a game for 2 players. Each of them has their own goal. There are 2 ways to win the game:

  • Get ball into opposite player's goal
  • Make opposite player not be able to do move

During each turn you move the ball by drawing lines between neighboring points.

You cannot move using existing segments, but if the ball touches already drawn line, you can continue moving.

Have a nice game!
